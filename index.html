What's For Supper

Project Inspiration!

The idea for this project started as a way to create a tracking system of what food I have in my fridge/pantry at home! The idea was to curate a selection of recipes based on what I have left to reduce food waste and trips to the grocery store.

The initial plan was to create a checklist of current groceries and use an API to search for recipes based on ingredient selections. I always wanted to have an option for a randomised search for when you may be stuck on what to eat!

As the project progressed, I opted to switch from the checklists to a search bar to create a more streamlined design. This meant shifting focus from tracking what's left at home to searching for recipes based on what I feel like eating! This has certainly been an exercise on exploring design elements and trying to improve my creativity.

All About Me!

My name is Jackie Barry and I grew up in Ottawa, Ontario. Following high school I moved to St. John’s NL to begin my academic adventure at Memorial university. The decision to move to Newfoundland was always an easy one- there’s just something about that ocean air that calls to me.

I’ve spent the last four years teaching in the province. Most of that time has been spent living in Happy Valley- Goose Bay, Labrador. It’s been amazing and I have had the opportunity to experience so many adventures!

Even though I’ve loved my time in Labrador, I decided it was time to start a new journey. I’ve always regretted not pursuing a career in the tech industry and realised that there is no reason why I can’t make that change now.

I started with the Get Coding program in February and have been learning the basics of HTML, CSS and Vanilla JavaScript in order to complete my first project!
